## What Was Watson?

IBM Watson was a question-answering computer system developed by IBM, named after IBM's founder Thomas J. Watson. Key facts:

- **Not a modern LLM**: Watson used DeepQA technology, not transformer-based neural networks
- **Rule-based + statistical**: Combined hundreds of language analysis algorithms
- **Domain-specific training**: Required extensive manual data preparation for each application
- **Eventually discontinued**: By 2023, Watson had cost IBM billions with limited commercial success

### Watson's Limitations in Cooking

According to NPR's 2015 coverage:
- Required significant human intervention to make recipes practical
- Suggestions were often bizarre or impractical
- Steve Abrams (IBM Watson director): *"Watson enhances people's ability to be creative and amplifies the process... The idea is not to find existing answers, but to find new insights that are worthy of trying."*
