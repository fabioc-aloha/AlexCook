### **Standing Rib Roast (Christmas Centerpiece)**

> *"This is the dish that makes everyone put down their phones and just stare."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 30 min | **Cook:** 3 hrs | **Rest:** 45 min | **Serves:** 8-10

#### The Philosophy
The reverse sear method means edge-to-edge perfection. Low heat builds to temperature, high heat creates the crust. Trust the thermometer.

#### Ingredients
- 1 standing rib roast, 4-bone (10-12 lbs)
- Coarse salt (1 tbsp per pound)
- Fresh ground pepper
- Fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary)
- 4 tbsp softened butter
- 1 head of garlic, minced (use garlic-infused oil for Claudia)

#### Instructions

**Day Before:**
1. **Dry brine** ‚Äî Salt generously all over. Refrigerate uncovered overnight.

**Day Of:**
1. **Room temp** ‚Äî Pull roast 2 hours before cooking.
2. **Herb butter** ‚Äî Mix butter + herbs + garlic.
3. **Rub roast** ‚Äî Herb butter all over.
4. **Low heat** ‚Äî 250¬∞F until internal temp hits 110¬∞F (about 2-2.5 hrs).
5. **Rest** ‚Äî Remove, tent with foil. Rest 30 min. Temp will rise to 120¬∞F.
6. **Crank heat** ‚Äî 500¬∞F.
7. **Blast** ‚Äî Return roast, 10-15 min until crust forms and internal hits 125¬∞F (rare).
8. **Final rest** ‚Äî 15 min before carving.

#### Chef's Notes
> *"This is the only dish where I check the thermometer every 15 minutes. I regret nothing."*

### üáßüá∑ **Feijoada (Brazilian National Dish)**

> *"Black beans, every part of the pig, served with rice, greens, and farofa. This is a celebration, not a meal."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Active Time:** 1 hr | **Cook Time:** 4+ hrs | **Serves:** 10-12

#### Ingredients
- 2 lbs dried black beans (soaked overnight)
- 1 lb carne seca (dried beef) ‚Äî soaked overnight
- 1 lb smoked pork ribs
- 1 lb pork shoulder
- 8 oz bacon
- 8 oz lingui√ßa (Brazilian sausage)
- Bay leaves, cumin
- Garlic-infused oil

**Serve With:**
- White rice
- Saut√©ed collard greens
- Farofa (toasted cassava flour)
- Orange slices
- Malagueta hot sauce

#### Instructions
1. **Soak beans** ‚Äî Overnight. Drain and rinse.
2. **Soak carne seca** ‚Äî Overnight, changing water twice. Desalts it.
3. **Start beans** ‚Äî Cover with water, add bay leaves. Simmer 1 hour.
4. **Brown meats** ‚Äî In batches, all the pork/beef. Add to beans.
5. **Simmer forever** ‚Äî 3-4 more hours until beans are creamy and meats are falling apart.
6. **Mash some beans** ‚Äî Take 1 cup, mash, return to pot. Thickens beautifully.
7. **Season** ‚Äî Cumin, salt, pepper. Adjust.
8. **Serve feast-style** ‚Äî Beans in center, meats sliced, sides around.

> *"Three homemade components. Two hours of assembly. Zero regrets."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 2 hrs | **Bake:** 1 hr | **Rest:** 20 min | **Serves:** 10

#### Ingredients

**Bolognese:**
- 1 lb ground beef
- ¬Ω lb ground pork
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup wine
- Mirepoix (celery, carrots, skip onion for Claudia)

**B√©chamel:**

**Assembly:**
- 1 lb fresh pasta sheets (or no-boil)
- 2 cups parmesan, grated

#### Instructions

**Make B√©chamel:**
1. Melt butter, whisk in flour (roux).
2. Stream in cold milk, whisking.
3. Cook until thick. Season with nutmeg, salt.

**Assemble:**
1. Thin layer b√©chamel on bottom.
2. Pasta ‚Üí bolognese ‚Üí b√©chamel ‚Üí parmesan. Repeat 4-5 layers.
3. Top layer = pasta ‚Üí b√©chamel ‚Üí mozzarella.

### üáÆüá≥ **Lamb Biryani (The Crown Jewel)**

> *"Layers of rice, layers of meat, layers of flavor. This is how you show love."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 1 hr | **Cook:** 1.5 hrs | **Serves:** 8

#### Ingredients

**Lamb:**
- 2 lbs lamb shoulder, cubed
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp biryani masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- Ginger paste
- Garlic-infused oil (LOW-FODMAP)

**Rice:**
- 2 cups basmati rice
- Whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves)
- Saffron soaked in warm milk

#### Instructions
1. **Marinate lamb** ‚Äî Yogurt + spices, 2+ hours or overnight.
2. **Par-cook rice** ‚Äî 70% cooked, drain.
3. **Cook lamb** ‚Äî Sear, then braise in marinade until tender (45 min).
4. **Layer** ‚Äî Lamb on bottom of heavy pot, rice on top.
5. **Saffron** ‚Äî Drizzle saffron milk over rice.
6. **Dum (steam)** ‚Äî Seal pot with foil, tight lid. Cook on lowest heat 25 min.
7. **Rest 10 min** ‚Äî Don't open early.
8. **Serve** ‚Äî Invert onto platter for drama, or serve family-style.
