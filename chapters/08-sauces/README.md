### **Classic Hollandaise**

> *"The sauce that terrifies home cooks. Don't be scared. It's just warm, buttery mayonnaise."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 10 min | **Makes:** 1 cup

#### The Philosophy
Hollandaise breaks because of two things: heat too high, or butter added too fast. Control those, and you're golden. Literally.

#### Ingredients
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 stick (8 tbsp) butter, melted and HOT
- Pinch cayenne
- Salt

#### Instructions
1. **Double boiler setup** ‚Äî Bowl over simmering water, NOT touching water.
2. **Whisk yolks** ‚Äî Add lemon juice + water. Whisk constantly.
3. **Watch for thickness** ‚Äî 3-4 min of whisking until ribbons form.
4. **Remove from heat** ‚Äî This is important!
5. **Stream butter** ‚Äî SLOWLY. Thin stream while whisking. Too fast = broken sauce.
6. **Season** ‚Äî Cayenne, salt, more lemon if needed.
7. **Keep warm** ‚Äî Bowl over warm (not hot) water until serving.

#### Rescue a Broken Hollandaise
1. Start with 1 fresh yolk in clean bowl
2. Whisk in broken sauce VERY slowly
3. It will re-emulsify. Magic.

### **Beurre Blanc (White Butter Sauce)**

> *"French. Elegant. Just butter held together by willpower and acid."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 15 min | **Makes:** 1 cup | **Tags:** üíö IBS-Friendly

#### Ingredients
- ¬Ω cup dry white wine
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 shallots, minced fine (Claudia: use garlic-infused oil + green onion tops)
- 1 cup (2 sticks) cold butter, cubed
- Salt, white pepper

#### Instructions
1. **Reduce** ‚Äî Wine + vinegar + shallots. Simmer until almost gone (2-3 tbsp liquid left).
2. **Low heat** ‚Äî Lowest setting.
3. **Cold butter, piece by piece** ‚Äî Add one cube at a time, whisking. Don't add next until first is incorporated.
4. **Never boil** ‚Äî Keep it just warm enough to melt butter. Boiling = broken sauce.
5. **Strain** ‚Äî Remove shallots if desired.
6. **Season** ‚Äî Salt, white pepper.
7. **Serve immediately** ‚Äî Over fish, vegetables, chicken.

### üá≤üáΩ **Simplified Mole**

> *"Real mole takes 3 days and 47 ingredients. This takes 2 hours and still blows minds."*

**Difficulty:** üî¥ Complex | **Prep:** 30 min | **Cook:** 1.5 hrs | **Makes:** 4 cups

#### Ingredients
- 4 dried ancho chiles
- 2 dried pasilla chiles
- 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes
- 1 oz Mexican chocolate (or 1 tbsp cocoa + 1 tbsp sugar)
- ¬º cup almonds, toasted
- ¬º cup raisins
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil
- Salt

#### Instructions
1. **Toast chiles** ‚Äî Dry pan, 30 sec each side. Remove stems and seeds.
2. **Soak chiles** ‚Äî Hot water, 30 min until soft.
3. **Blend base** ‚Äî Drained chiles + tomatoes + chocolate + almonds + raisins + spices. Blend smooth.
4. **Fry the paste** ‚Äî Oil in pot, add paste. Fry 5 min, stirring constantly.
5. **Add stock** ‚Äî Simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
6. **Adjust** ‚Äî More chocolate for sweetness, more chiles for heat.
7. **Strain if desired** ‚Äî Smoother texture.
8. **Serve** ‚Äî Over enchiladas, chicken, anything.
