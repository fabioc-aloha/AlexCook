### **Classic Hollandaise**

> *"The sauce that terrifies home cooks. Don't be scared. It's just warm, buttery mayonnaise."*

**Difficulty:** ğŸ”´ Complex | **Prep:** 5 min | **Cook:** 10 min | **Makes:** 1 cup

#### The Philosophy
Hollandaise breaks because of two things: heat too high, or butter added too fast. Control those, and you're golden. Literally.

#### Ingredients
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 stick (8 tbsp) butter, melted and HOT
- Pinch cayenne
- Salt

#### Instructions
1. **Double boiler setup** â€” Bowl over simmering water, NOT touching water.
2. **Whisk yolks** â€” Add lemon juice + water. Whisk constantly.
3. **Watch for thickness** â€” 3-4 min of whisking until ribbons form.
4. **Remove from heat** â€” This is important!
5. **Stream butter** â€” SLOWLY. Thin stream while whisking. Too fast = broken sauce.
6. **Season** â€” Cayenne, salt, more lemon if needed.
7. **Keep warm** â€” Bowl over warm (not hot) water until serving.

#### Rescue a Broken Hollandaise
1. Start with 1 fresh yolk in clean bowl
2. Whisk in broken sauce VERY slowly
3. It will re-emulsify. Magic.
